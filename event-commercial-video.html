Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

An event recap captures something that's happening live — a gala, festival, game, or celebration. We're documenting real moments as they unfold. A commercial or promo video is a produced piece with planned shots, scripting, and a specific message designed to promote your brand, product, or service. Both are short-form and cinematic, but the process is different.

Our standard deliverable is a 30-second to 2-minute short-form video. This is the sweet spot for social media, websites, and audience retention. If you need a longer 3–5 minute cut, we offer that as an add-on for $300.

For events, we recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance to secure your date. For commercial projects, earlier is better since we'll need time for pre-production planning. That said, we can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines — just reach out and we'll see what we can do.

Standard turnaround is 2–3 weeks from the shoot date. This gives us time to edit, color grade, and deliver a polished final product. If you need it faster, our same-week delivery add-on ($200) gets you the final edit within 5 business days.

Absolutely. Our Social Media Cuts add-on ($150) includes additional 15- and 30-second edits formatted specifically for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories. These are pulled from the main video footage so everything stays cohesive.

Yes — concept development and scripting assistance is included with every commercial and promo video. We'll work with you to define the message, plan the shots, and make sure the final product aligns with your brand goals. You bring the vision, we bring the execution.
